Three (eleven) years of February

published2025-02-23
reading time1 min
categorieskaleidoscope

Last year the feeling was of a quiet annoyance: “we stand together” was all about standing, not winning. This year, well, I’m pissed. Angry, osti de calisse de tabarnak.

It’s a truly marvelous sight to witness, the death of the empire. Not the best time for that, though.

Ukraine will win, of course. That’s just how it works. Alas, it would be preferable not to loose so many and so much.

As, for, unexpectedly, Canada, I’m less optimistic. How ironic that I moved from Northern Kazakhstan bordering crazy annexation-hungry country to Canada… also bordering crazy annexation-hungry country.
